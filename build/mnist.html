Handwritten digits

One of the simplest examples of image classification; low-resolution handwritten digits. Try it out!

Predictions are near-instant. Delays are for better UX; drawing a 4 for example :)

Classifiers like this one use a softmax function. You can think of it a probability distribution; it gives a value for each label, and together they sum up to one. You then typically take the label with the highest value and consider that your classification. You can also use that value for reasoning about the AI's confidence for example.
